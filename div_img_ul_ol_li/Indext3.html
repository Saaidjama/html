Ahmed Shire 'Lawaaxe'

Ahmed Shire 'Lawaaxe' was the fist Somali to graduate from a westren university.

Short Biography:

  • He is noted as the first Somali man to graduate from a Western university.
  • e served as an administrator and organizer within the Somali Youth League (SYL).
  • He later became Chief of Protocol under President Abdirashid Ali Sharmarke.

Other Positions He Held:

  1. President of the Somali National Academy of Culture
  2. Founder of the National Magazine “The Light of Knowledge and Education” (Iftiinka Aqoonta)
  3. Linguist and Early Campaigner for Latin Orthography
